Butter Chicken

A rich and creamy dish made with tender chicken pieces cooked in a spiced tomato sauce.

Ingredients:

  1. Chicken
  2. Butter
  3. Garlic
  4. Ginger
  5. Onion
  6. Tomatoes
  7. Heavy Cream
  8. Spices (such as garam masala, turmeric, and chili powder)
  9. Salt
  10. Cilantro (for garnish)

Biryani

A fragrant and flavorful rice dish made with basmati rice, spices, and either chicken, mutton, or fish.

Ingredients:

  1. Rice
  2. Meat (chicken, mutton, or fish)
  3. Cumin
  4. Coriander
  5. Turmeric
  6. Red chili powder
  7. Vegetables

Paneer Tikka

A popular Indian appetizer made with marinated paneer (Indian cottage cheese) grilled to perfection.

Ingredients:

  1. Paneer (Indian cottage cheese)
  2. Yogurt
  3. Ginger-garlic paste
  4. Spices (such as cumin powder, coriander powder, turmeric, and red chili powder)
  5. Lemon juice
  6. Vegetables (bell peppers, onions, tomatoes)
  7. Oil
  8. Salt
  9. Chaat masala (for garnish)
  10. Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish)
